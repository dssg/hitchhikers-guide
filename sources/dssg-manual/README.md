# The DSSG Manual
Welcome to the [Data Science for Social Good](https://www.dssgfellowship.org/) (DSSG) Fellowship program! We hope your experience this summer will help you grow your skills as a data scientist and learn how to apply those skills to solve real-world problems with social impact. This manual outlines our goals in running this fellowship program, our hopes for your experience, and our expectations of the participants.
